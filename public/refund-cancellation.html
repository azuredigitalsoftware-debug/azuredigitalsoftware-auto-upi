1. Non-Refundable Products

Once a digital product has been delivered and the activation key has been provided, the sale is generally final. This is because:

  • Digital products can be instantly distributed and cannot be "returned"
  • Activation keys are unique and single-use
  • Products are delivered immediately upon payment confirmation

We will consider refunds in the following circumstances:

3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will NOT be issued for:

  • Change of mind or purchasing by mistake
  • Product does not meet your expectations
  • You changed your system or device after purchase
  • You chose the wrong product
  • The key was already used before requesting a refund
  • You violate the Terms of Use or licensing agreements

4. Refund Process

To request a refund, follow these steps:

  1. Contact us within 30 days of your purchase with:
    • Your order number
    • Email address used for purchase
    • Reason for refund request
    • Any relevant supporting evidence
  2. We will review your request and respond within 3-5 business days
  3. If approved, your refund will be processed to the original payment method
  4. Refunds typically appear within 5-10 business days, depending on your bank

5. Cancellation Policy

Before Delivery

If you wish to cancel your order BEFORE the product is delivered, you may request cancellation within 1 hour of purchase. Contact us immediately with your order details, and we will cancel and issue a full refund.

After Delivery

Once the product has been delivered, cancellation is not possible. However, see the "Eligible for Refund" section above for circumstances where we will process a refund.

6. Subscription Products

If applicable, subscription-based products can be cancelled anytime. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle. No refunds are issued for partial months of unused service.

7. Refund Disputes

If you dispute a charge with your bank or payment provider (chargeback), we reserve the right to:

  • Deactivate your product license
  • Dispute the chargeback with evidence of delivery
  • Suspend your account from future purchases

Please attempt to resolve issues with us before filing disputes with payment providers.

8. Contact for Refund Requests

To request a refund or cancellation, contact us with your order details. We'll respond within 2-4 business hours.
