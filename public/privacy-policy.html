We use third-party services for payment processing, email delivery, and analytics. These services have their own privacy policies. We do not share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to:

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies help us:

You can control cookie settings in your browser. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

6. Retention of Information

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

To exercise these rights, contact us.

Our services are not intended for children under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we discover that we have collected data from a child, we will delete it immediately.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us and we'll respond within 2-4 business hours.
